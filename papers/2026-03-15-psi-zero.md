# Ψ₀ (Psi-Zero): An Open Foundation Model Towards Universal Humanoid Loco-Manipulation

- **arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12263
- **PDF**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.12263
- **Categories**: cs.RO
- **Published**: 2026-03-12

## Authors
Songlin Wei, Hongyi Jing, Boqian Li, Zhenyu Zhao, Jiageng Mao, et al.

## Abstract
We introduce **Ψ₀ (Psi-Zero)**, an **open foundation model** to address challenging humanoid loco-manipulation tasks.

## Problem
While existing approaches often attempt to address this fundamental problem by co-training on large and diverse human and humanoid data, we argue that this strategy is **suboptimal** due to the fundamental **kinematic and motion disparities** between humans and humanoid robots. Therefore, data efficiency and model performance remain unsatisfactory despite the considerable data volume.

## Solution: Decoupled Learning
To address this challenge, Ψ₀ **decouples the learning process** to maximize the utility of heterogeneous data:

### Architecture
1. **Human motion understanding**: Learns from large-scale human motion data
2. **Humanoid-specific adaptation**: Transfers to humanoid kinematics
3. **Task-specific fine-tuning**: Adapts to specific loco-manipulation tasks

### Key Benefits
- **Data-efficient**: Better use of available human and humanoid data
- **Generalizable**: Foundation model approach enables transfer
- **Open**: Model and weights will be released

## Capabilities
- Whole-body loco-manipulation
- Coordinated walking and manipulation
- Object interaction while moving

## Why Notable
- **Open foundation model**: First open model for humanoid loco-manipulation
- **Novel decoupling approach**: Addresses human-humanoid motion gap
- **Timely**: Humanoid robotics rapidly advancing
