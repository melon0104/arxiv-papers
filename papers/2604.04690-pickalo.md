# Pickalo: Leveraging 6D Pose Estimation for Low-Cost Industrial Bin Picking

**arXiv ID:** 2604.04690  
**Category:** cs.RO  
**Published:** 2026-04-06

## Authors
Alessandro Tarsi, Matteo Mastrogiuseppe, Saverio Taliani

## Abstract
Bin picking in real industrial environments remains challenging due to severe clutter, occlusions, and the high cost of traditional 3D sensing setups. We present Pickalo, a modular 6D pose-based bin-picking pipeline built entirely on low-cost hardware. A wrist-mounted RGB-D camera actively explores the scene from multiple viewpoints, while raw stereo streams are processed with BridgeDepth to obtain refined depth maps suitable for accurate collision reasoning.
