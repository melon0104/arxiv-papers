# Beyond Hard Negatives: The Importance of Score Distribution in Knowledge Distillation for Dense Retrieval

**arXiv ID:** 2604.04734  
**Category:** cs.IR

## Authors
Youngjoon Jang, Seongtae Hong, Hyeonseok Moon

## Abstract
Transferring knowledge from a cross-encoder teacher via Knowledge Distillation (KD) has become a standard paradigm for training retrieval models. While existing studies have largely focused on mining hard negatives to improve discrimination, the systematic composition of training data and the resulting teacher score distribution have received relatively less attention.
