# AvatarPointillist: AutoRegressive 4D Gaussian Avatarization

**arXiv ID:** 2604.04787  
**Category:** cs.CV  
**Published:** 2026-04-06

## Authors
Hongyu Liu, Xuan Wang, Yating Wang

## Abstract
We introduce AvatarPointillist, a novel framework for generating dynamic 4D Gaussian avatars from a single portrait image. At the core of our method is a decoder-only Transformer that autoregressively generates a point cloud for 3D Gaussian Splatting. This sequential approach allows for precise, adaptive construction, dynamically adjusting point density and the total number of points based on the subject's complexity.
