# Measuring Generalization in Vision Transformers Through Inner Workings

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08192  
**Category:** cs.LG  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Peng, Yunxiang, Li, Yifan, Wang, Ziqi, Li, Weijie, Gao, Yu, Fan, Jianping

## Abstract
We introduce circuit-based proxy metrics for generalization under distribution shift: Dependency Depth Bias for model selection and Circuit Shift Score for post-deployment monitoring. These correlate better with true performance than confidence-based metrics. CVPR 2026 Highlight; code: https://github.com/deep-real/GenCircuit.

## Key Contributions
- モデル内部回路を使ったラベル不要の汎化指標を提案。
- 配布前評価と配布後モニタリングの両方に対応。
