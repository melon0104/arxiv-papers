# Grounding Clinical AI Competency in Human Cognition Through the Clinical World Model and Skill-Mix Framework

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08226  
**Category:** cs.AI  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Safavi-Naini, Seyed Amir Ahmad, Meftah, Elahe, Mohess, Josh, Mohammadi Kazaj, Pooya, Siontis, Georgios, Atf, Zahra, Lewis, Peter R., Reyes, Mauricio, Nadkarni, Girish, Wiest, Roland, Windecker, Stephan, Grani, Christoph, Soroush, Ali, Shiri, Isaac

## Abstract
We introduce the Clinical World Model that formalizes patient-provider-ecosystem interactions, and the Clinical AI Skill-Mix with eight dimensions to define competency coordinates. The framework argues that validation does not transfer across coordinates and provides a common grammar for evaluation. Code/data: https://github.com/Sdamirsa/Clinical-World-Model.

## Key Contributions
- 臨床AIの能力を患者・医療者・環境の三者モデルで定式化。
- 8次元Skill-Mixで評価座標を定義し、汎化の限界を明確化。
- 仕様/ダッシュボードを公開。
