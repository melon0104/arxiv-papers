# Distributed Multi-Layer Editing for Rule-Level Knowledge in Large Language Models

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08284  
**Category:** cs.CL  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Sun, Haoliang, Wang, Jialin, Pan, Yu, Liang, Qun

## Abstract
We study rule-level knowledge editing and show that rules distribute across layers (formulas/descriptions early, instances mid). DMLE applies coordinated edits across layers, improving rule understanding and instance portability on GPT-J, Qwen, and LLaMA. Code: https://github.com/Pepper66/DMLE.

## Key Contributions
- RuleEditベンチを200ルールへ拡張し、規則知識の層内分布を解析。
- Distributed Multi-Layer Editingで規則編集の一貫性を向上。
- 複数モデルで規則理解/移植性を大幅改善、コード公開。
