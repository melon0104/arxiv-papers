# Selective Latent Reasoning in Large Language Models

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08299  
**Category:** cs.CL  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Fu, Renyu, Li, Qing, Zhang, Jing, Li, Wenjie, Zhang, Min, Zhang, Yue

## Abstract
SeLaR is a training-free latent reasoning method that activates soft embeddings only at low-confidence steps and regularizes them to avoid collapse. It outperforms standard CoT and prior training-free methods on five reasoning benchmarks. Camera-ready for ACL 2026.
