# SUPERNOVA: Eliciting General Reasoning in LLMs with Reinforcement Learning on Natural Instructions

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08477  
**Category:** cs.AI  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Suvarna, Ashima, Wang, Aohan, Zhang, Xinyu, Gandhi, Ashutosh, Ghosh, Shubham

## Abstract
SUPERNOVA curates RLVR data from natural instruction datasets to improve general reasoning beyond math/code. It studies data design via 100+ RL experiments and achieves large gains on BBEH, Zebralogic, and MMLU-Pro. Code: https://github.com/asuvarna31/supernova.

## Key Contributions
- 自然言語指示データからRLVR向けデータを設計・抽出する枠組み。
- データ選択/混合/介入の設計要因を大規模に検証。
- 汎用推論ベンチで大幅改善、コード公開。
