# Fail2Drive: Benchmarking Closed-Loop Driving Generalization

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08535  
**Category:** cs.RO  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Renz, Katrin, Zhao, Wenqiang, Wu, Haodong, Althoff, Matthias

## Abstract
Fail2Drive is a paired-route benchmark for closed-loop autonomous driving generalization in CARLA, with 200 routes and 17 shift classes. It shows an average success drop of 22.8% and releases code/data at https://github.com/autonomousvision/fail2drive.

## Key Contributions
- 分布シフトを分離評価できるpaired-routeベンチマークを構築。
- 一般化性能の大幅劣化（平均-22.8%）を定量化。
- シナリオ生成ツールとデータを公開。
