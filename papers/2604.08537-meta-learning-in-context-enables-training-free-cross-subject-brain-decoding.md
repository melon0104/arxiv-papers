# Meta-learning In-Context Enables Training-Free Cross Subject Brain Decoding

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08537  
**Category:** cs.LG  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Nan, Mu, Yu, Muquan, Mai, Weijian, Prince, Jacob S., Adeli, Hossein, Zhang, Rui, Cao, Jiahang, Becker, Benjamin, Pyles, John A., Henderson, Margaret M., Song, Chunfeng, Kriegeskorte, Nikolaus, Tarr, Michael J., Hu, Xiaoqing, Luo, Andrew F.

## Abstract
We present a meta-optimized in-context approach for cross-subject fMRI decoding that generalizes without fine-tuning. The method infers subject-specific encoders from a small context and achieves strong cross-scanner generalization. Accepted to CVPR 2026; code at https://github.com/ezacngm/brainCodec.

## Key Contributions
- 少数の脳活動例から新被験者のエンコーディングを推定し、ゼロ微調整で一般化。
- 階層的推論でボクセル応答と視覚表現を同時に推定。
- CVPR 2026採択、コード公開。
