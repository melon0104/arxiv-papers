# ParseBench: A Document Parsing Benchmark for AI Agents

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08538  
**Category:** cs.CV  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Zhang, Boyang, Liu, Jerry, Zhang, Ziyang, Liu, Jerry, Chen, Luyu

## Abstract
ParseBench is a benchmark of ~2,000 enterprise document pages focusing on semantic correctness for agentic parsing. It evaluates tables, charts, content faithfulness, semantic formatting, and visual grounding. Dataset and code: https://huggingface.co/datasets/llamaindex/ParseBench and https://github.com/run-llama/ParseBench.

## Key Contributions
- エージェント向け文書解析のための新ベンチ（約2,000ページ）。
- 表/図/意味忠実性など5能力軸で評価。
- データセットと評価コードを公開。
