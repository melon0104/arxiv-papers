# Aligning Textual Numerals and Visual Instances in Text-to-Video Diffusion Models

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08546  
**Category:** cs.CV  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Sun, Zhengyang, Li, Yuhan, Xu, Chao, Chen, Yu, Liu, Yuxuan

## Abstract
NUMINA is a training-free identify-then-guide framework that improves numerical alignment in text-to-video diffusion. It builds a countable latent layout and refines it to guide regeneration, improving CountBench accuracy. CVPR 2026; code: https://github.com/H-EmbodVis/NUMINA.

## Key Contributions
- 数指定プロンプトの失敗を検出しレイアウトを修正するtraining-free手法。
- CountBenchで最大+7.4%のカウント精度改善。
- CVPR 2026採択、コード公開。
