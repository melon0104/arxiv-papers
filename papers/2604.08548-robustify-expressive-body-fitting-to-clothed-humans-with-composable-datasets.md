# Robustify Expressive Body Fitting to Clothed Humans with Composable Datasets

**arXiv ID:** 2604.08548  
**Category:** cs.CV  
**Published:** 2026-04-09

## Authors
Li, Xiaoben, Xu, Zhenhua, Wang, Yuxuan, Zhang, Huamin, Zhang, Xuaner

## Abstract
ETCH-X upgrades ETCH for robust, expressive body fitting from clothed point clouds. It introduces tightness-aware undressing, SMPL-X expressiveness, and dense correspondences trained on composable datasets. Code: https://github.com/XiaobenLi00/ETCH-X.

## Key Contributions
- 衣服ダイナミクスを除去するundressと密対応のdense fitを導入。
- SMPL-Xで手・顔などの表現性を強化しロバスト化。
- コード公開、複数データで大幅改善。
