## Abstract
Real-time detection and mitigation of technical anomalies are critical for large-scale cloud-native services, where even minutes of downtime can result in massive financial losses and diminished user trust. We present TingIS, a production system for real-time risk event discovery from noisy customer incident reports at enterprise scale. TingIS leverages LLMs to extract structured risk events from unstructured, high-volume incident streams, enabling rapid response and root cause analysis. The system has been deployed in production environments handling millions of service interactions daily.
