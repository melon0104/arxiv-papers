# UniGenDet: A Unified Generative-Discriminative Framework for Co-Evolutionary Image Generation and Generated Image Detection

**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21904
**公開日:** 2026-04-23
**カテゴリ:** cs.CV

## 著者

## 概要（日本語）
画像生成と生成画像検出は独立して発展してきたが、本研究はこの2つを**共進化（co-evolutionary）**させる統一フレームワーク「UniGenDet」を提案。生成モデルが検出器を欺こうとすることで生成品質が向上し、検出器が高品質な偽造画像を学習することで検出精度が向上するという相互強化サイクルを実現。

- ディープフェイク対策・コンテンツ認証に応用可能

## Abstract
In recent years, significant progress has been made in both image generation and generated image detection. Despite their rapid, yet largely independent development, these two fields have evolved disconnected from each other. We argue that generation and detection should co-evolve in a unified framework. UniGenDet couples a generative model with a discriminative detector in a competitive training loop: the generator learns to produce increasingly realistic images that fool the detector, while the detector is trained on progressively harder synthetic examples. This co-evolutionary dynamic improves both generation quality and detection robustness simultaneously.
