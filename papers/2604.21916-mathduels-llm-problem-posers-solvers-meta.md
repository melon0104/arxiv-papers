## 概要（日本語）
最先端LLMが静的数学ベンチマークで天井近いスコアを達成しつつある問題に対応。LLMが「問題作成者」と「解答者」の両役を担うセルフプレイ方式の**動的数学ベンチマーク**「MathDuels」を提案。問題作成能力と解答能力を同時評価することで、静的ベンチマークでは差別化できないモデル間の能力差を明確化する。

- 数学推論能力の新たな評価パラダイム

## Abstract
As frontier language models attain near-ceiling performance on static mathematical benchmarks, existing evaluations are increasingly unable to differentiate model capabilities, largely because they rely on fixed problem sets that models may have memorized. We introduce MathDuels, a dynamic evaluation framework where LLMs act as both problem posers and solvers in a self-play setting. By evaluating the quality of generated problems and the accuracy of solutions simultaneously, MathDuels reveals capability gaps between models that static benchmarks miss, providing a more robust and contamination-resistant assessment of mathematical reasoning.
